About

I am a graduating mechanical and chemical engineer with minors in computer science and entrepreneurship and innovation. I have 8 years of machine shop and CAD design experience with proficiency in several CAD programs. I'm also a passionate design thinker, lucky enough to be a member of many design teams.

My work in these fields has led me to:

  • Design a polyethylene plant with a 6 person team
  • Work with a team to create a musical wearable for students in the local deaf community
  • Work with a team to design a discrete medicine storage container that assists users in tracking their doses
  • Design an interaface for visualizing the effects of sound mixing on waveforms
