About

Eli is majoring in Chemical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering, and minoring in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh.

He moved to Pittsburgh from coastal Southwest Florida. Eli enjoys playing billiards, going rock climbing, being active in musical theatre. He also loves makerspaces and machine shops, having made several projects in and dedicated numerous volunteer hours to such organizations. His academic interests vary widely: including prosthetics, molecular biology, programming, organic chemistry, and general mechanical design. Eli enjoys challenging himself to learn new subjects and push his understanding of what he is familiar with.
